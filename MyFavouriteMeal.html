Noodles Recipe

About Noodles

We have different companies who produce noodles all over the world but we have 3 popular productions in my country which are Indomie,Mimee and chikki,while in korea Ramen is the most popular noodles.

S/N Ingredients Quantity
1 Noodles 1 Pack
2 pepper 2 bells
3 Water just enough
4 Onion small onions
5 green pepper as preferred
6 cabbage just a little
7 Sesame just a pinch
8 Groundnut oil 1 tsp
9 carrot 2 chopped carrots
10 mixed vegetables as many as you like
11 Egg 2

Preparation

  • Chop your onions,pepper,vegetables,cabbage
  • Pour a little water in a pot and boil
  • Put the noodles into the boiling water
  • Cook noodles till its soft
  • Drain the water and put the noodles in a pot
  • Pour 1 tsp of oil into the pot
  • Fry your chopped ingredients till they are enough
  • Then add the sauce along with them
  • Stir till its properly blended
  • Pour the noodles you boiled
  • Fry and stir noodles till they have mixed well
  • Leave on fire for 2 minutes
  • Fry your egg
  • Food is ready , Serve as desired
